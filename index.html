Practice Areas

Corporate Law

Comprehensive legal advice for businesses of all sizes, from startups to established corporations. Services include entity formation, contract drafting and review, mergers and acquisitions, and compliance matters.

Estate Planning

Protect your assets and provide for your loved ones with carefully crafted estate plans. Services include wills, trusts, powers of attorney, healthcare directives, and probate assistance.

Real Estate Law

Navigate complex property transactions with confidence. Services include residential and commercial closings, lease agreements, title issues, property disputes, and zoning matters.

Civil Litigation

Strong representation in civil disputes, with a strategic approach to achieving favorable outcomes. Services include case evaluation, settlement negotiation, discovery, and trial advocacy.

Family Law

Compassionate guidance through sensitive family matters. Services include divorce, child custody, spousal support, adoption, and domestic violence protection.

Personal Injury

Advocating for fair compensation for injury victims. Services include case evaluation, insurance negotiation, settlement discussions, and litigation when necessary.

About Me

Stanley Currell

With over 15 years of legal experience, I have dedicated my career to providing exceptional legal representation to individuals and businesses alike. After graduating with honors from UCL, I honed my skills at prestigious firms before establishing my own practice.

My approach combines thorough legal knowledge with a practical understanding of my clients' needs. I believe in accessibility, transparency, and building lasting relationships with those I represent.

Professional affiliations include membership in the State Bar Association, American Bar Association, and several practice-specific legal organizations. I regularly participate in continuing legal education to stay at the forefront of legal developments.

When not practicing law, I enjoy volunteering with local community organizations, hiking with my family, and mentoring aspiring attorneys.
